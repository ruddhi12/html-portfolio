Ruddhida's Top Three Favorite Movies

These are my go to films of all time!

Titanic

I enjoyed Titanic for its sweeping romance and epic portrayal of historical events, combined with powerful performances and stunning visuals.

The Pursuit of Happyness

The Pursuit of Happyness tells the inspiring story of a father's struggle against homelessness, featuring Will Smith's powerful performance.

The Dark Knight

The Dark Knight is a gripping, intense superhero film with exceptional performances, particularly Heath Ledger's iconic portrayal of the Joker, and a complex, thought-provoking narrative that elevates the genre.
